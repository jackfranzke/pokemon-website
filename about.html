About Me!

I started collecting Pokemon cards a few years back when my grandma would do some shopping for Target and every time we went there, I would always ask for a small Pokemon pack and she would say yes. This kept repeating for one year and my parents decided to get me a metal box full of Pokemon cards and I was excited about it. I only collect in small packs now and I don't like to battle with them because I don't know how to battle.

There are only a few things that I actually especially like and they are: games, puzzles, and connect the dots. I like games because they're a great way to deal with stress. What do I mean by that? Well, by absolutely demolishing every single enemy in your sight. Then, I like puzzles because I think they're fun and gives me a challenge and I like to complete challenges. Lastly, I enjoy connect the dots because you are slowly creating something by connecting dots like for example, you might connect dots into the shape of a dog or a cat.

Other Stuff I Like: Taekwondo!
