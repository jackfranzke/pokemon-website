Special Pokemon Cards

These Pokemon cards are special due to the type of cards they are (EX, GX, etc.) and also because some of them are Mega Evolutions. Mega Evolutions are temporary evolutions that require a special stone. This evolution only lasts for one hour, and when that hour is up, the Pokemon reverts to its original form.
